pizza

the details

This gourmet veggie and almond "ricotta" pizza is the ultimate veggie pizza! It's packed with big flavor and big nutrition–the perfect comfort food. Make it your own with your choice of veggies, or follow our lead and top it with spinach, yellow bell pepper, garlic, caramelized onion, and grape tomatoes. Who knew veggies could taste this good?

fixings

  • Pizza dough (pre-made or homemade)
  • 1/4 cup pizza sauce
  • 3/4 cup almond "ricotta"
  • 2 tsp olive oil
  • 1 1/2 cup baby spinach
  • 1 yellow bell pepper (sliced)
  • 3 cloves garlic (sliced)
  • 1 medium red onion (sliced & caramelized)
  • 1/4 cup grape tomatoes (sliced in half)
  • Salt & pepper to taste

make it happen

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F.
  2. On a pizza pan, roll out pizza dough and form it into a circle (approx. 12 inches). Roll and pinch the edges to form a crust.
  3. Spread pizza sauce evenly over dough.
  4. In a medium skillet, heat oil on medium heat and saute garlic, bell pepper, and onion. Once garlic is fragrant, remove from heat and add spinach. Toss to blend with other ingredients. Add salt/pepper to taste.
  5. Spread spinach/veggie mixture to pizza and spread evenly.
  6. Add almond "ricotta" and grape tomatoes on top.
  7. Bake for about 20 minutes. Enjoy!
