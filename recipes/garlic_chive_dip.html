garlic & chive dip

the details

This garlic and chive dip is perfect for a crowd. Its base is an almond "ricotta," which requires just minutes to make. From there, it's as easy as mincing some garlic, juicing a lemon, and heating the oven. Just be sure to include a "Contains Nuts" sign, otherwise your crowd will think it's made with dairy!

fixings

  • 2 cups almond "ricotta"
  • 1 large lemon (zested and juiced)
  • 2 large cloved garlic (minced)
  • 1 ounce fresh chives (minced)
  • 1 tsp sea salt
  • 1 tsp freshly ground black pepper

make it happen

  1. Heat the oven to 375°F. Lightly grease a 1/2- to 1-quart gratin dish or baking pan.
  2. Mix the ricotta, lemon juice and zest, garlic, chives, and salt, and black pepper. Spread in the baking dish and drizzle with olive oil. (At this point you can cover and refrigerate the ricotta overnight, if desired.)
  3. Bake for 15 to 20 minutes or until hot. Drizzle again with olive oil. Serve immediately with crackers for dipping.
